Our Leadership

Meet the experienced team leading JHA Arabia's operations in Saudi Arabia

Mr. Wang Zheng

General Manager

Mr. Wang Zheng brings over 15 years of extensive experience in airport construction and engineering management. As the General Manager of JHA Arabia, he leads the company's operations in Saudi Arabia, overseeing major airport infrastructure projects across the Kingdom.

His expertise spans visual navigation systems, air traffic control engineering, and large-scale airport construction management. Prior to his role at JHA Arabia, Mr. Wang held senior positions at Beijing Jinghang'an Technology Co., Ltd., where he managed critical airport projects including 4F-level airport constructions and non-stop flight condition implementations.

His technical proficiency combined with cross-cultural business acumen makes him uniquely qualified to lead JHA Arabia's expansion in the Middle Eastern aviation market.

  • Project management of international airport projects exceeding $500M
  • Technical leadership in visual navigation and ATC systems
  • Cross-cultural business development in Middle East markets
