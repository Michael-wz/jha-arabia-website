Our Services

Comprehensive airport construction and engineering solutions for Saudi Arabia's aviation sector

Comprehensive Airport Solutions

JHA Arabia provides a full range of airport construction and engineering services, leveraging over 20 years of expertise from our parent company in China.

Visual Navigation Engineering

Runway & Approach Lighting Systems

Specialized in airport visual guidance systems that ensure safe aircraft operations during all weather conditions and times of day.

Key Capabilities:

  • Runway edge lighting systems
  • Approach lighting systems (ALS)
  • Threshold and runway end lights
  • Taxiway guidance lighting
  • Precision approach path indicators (PAPI)
  • Visual docking guidance systems

Technical Standards:

  • ICAO Annex 14 compliance
  • FAA AC 150/5345 standards
  • LED technology implementation
  • Energy-efficient solutions

Air Traffic Control Engineering

Communication, Navigation & Surveillance

Complete ATC system installation and integration for safe and efficient air traffic management.

Key Capabilities:

  • ATC tower equipment installation
  • Voice communication systems (VCS)
  • Radar systems installation
  • Navigation aids (VOR, ILS, DME)
  • Automatic dependent surveillance (ADS-B)
  • Meteorological equipment

Technical Standards:

  • ICAO Doc 8071 compliance
  • EUROCAE ED-137 standards
  • Redundant system design
  • 24/7 operational reliability

  • Security surveillance systems (CCTV)
  • Access control systems
  • Fire alarm and detection systems
  • Public address systems
  • Structured cabling networks
  • Building management systems

Technical Standards:

  • ISO 27001 security standards
  • NFPA fire safety codes
  • TIA/EIA cabling standards
  • Integrated system design

Airfield Pavement Engineering

Runway & Taxiway Construction

Key Capabilities:

  • Taxiway and apron pavement
  • Pavement condition assessment
  • Non-destructive testing
  • Pavement management systems

  • Heavy-duty pavement solutions

Installation and maintenance of critical airport mechanical and electrical systems.
