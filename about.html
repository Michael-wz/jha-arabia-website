Parent company context

Parent-Platform Technical Strength

Founded in 2002 with registered capital of RMB 200 million, Beijing Jinghang'An Airport Engineering Co., Ltd. is a specialized civil airport engineering company. Official materials state that it holds 13 engineering qualifications and has participated in more than 100 airports at home and abroad.

Founded 2002 RMB 200 million 13 engineering qualifications
  • Airport visual navaids engineering and airfield lighting systems.
  • Civil aviation ATC, navigation, communication, and airport ELV systems.
  • Airport runway engineering, maintenance works, and electromechanical installation support.
  • Project references include Beijing Daxing, Shanghai Pudong, Qingdao Jiaodong, Ndola, and other major airports.
