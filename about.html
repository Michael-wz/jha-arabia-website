About JHA Arabia

Bringing Chinese airport construction expertise to Saudi Arabia's aviation sector

Our Story

JHA Arabia Co., Ltd. is the Saudi Arabian subsidiary of Beijing Jinghang'an Technology Co., Ltd. (JHA), a leading airport construction company with over 20 years of experience in China's aviation infrastructure sector.

Established to serve the growing aviation needs of the Middle East, JHA Arabia combines Chinese technical expertise with local Saudi knowledge to deliver world-class airport construction projects.

Our parent company, JHA, has been involved in numerous major airport projects across China and has developed specialized capabilities in 4F-level airport construction and non-stop flight condition operations.

Our Parent Company

Beijing Jinghang'an Technology Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2001, Beijing Jinghang'an Technology Co., Ltd. (JHA) is a comprehensive airport construction enterprise with qualifications in multiple specialized fields including:

  • Airport Visual Navigation Engineering
  • Civil Aviation Air Traffic Control Engineering
  • Airport Weak Current Systems Engineering
  • Airport Pavement Engineering
  • Electromechanical Equipment Installation

JHA became a wholly-owned subsidiary of Far East Smart Energy Co., Ltd. (Stock Code: 600869) in 2017, further strengthening its financial and technical capabilities.
