Core capabilities

Specialist Airport Engineering, Structured Clearly

Drawing on the parent company's official business scope, JHA Arabia focuses on airport visual navaids, civil aviation ATC engineering, airport ELV systems, and airfield support packages for Saudi aviation projects.

Visual Navigation

Lighting systems that support safe aircraft movement, runway guidance, and reliable night or low-visibility operations.

  • Runway and taxiway lighting packages
  • Approach lighting and PAPI systems
  • Control, monitoring, and commissioning support

ATC Engineering

Specialist systems for communication, navigation, surveillance, and operational readiness within critical airport environments.

  • ATC tower system packages
  • Navigation aids and support equipment
  • Testing, interfaces, and redundancy planning

Weak Current Systems

  • CCTV, access control, and structured cabling
  • Fire alarm and PA integration
  • Operations-focused low-voltage infrastructure

Airfield Pavement

Support for runway, taxiway, apron, and pavement rehabilitation scopes where sequencing and operational disruption matter.

  • Runway and taxiway rehabilitation support
  • Condition assessment and staging logic
  • Interface planning around active operations
